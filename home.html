Instructions

  • The first player turns over two cards.
  • If the two cards match, the player earns points and gets another turn to try for another match.
  • If the two cards do not match, they are turned face down again.
  • The next player then takes a turn.
  • Players continue taking turns until all matches are found.
  • The player with the most matches at the end of the game wins.
